  • Ollie
    • The Ollie is the easiest trick. Here is how you perform an olie...
      1. Pop the tail with back foot.
      2. As you pop the tail, twist your foot to be perpendicular to the board.
      3. Slide the perpendicular foot across the board.
      4. Jump as you flick your foot off the edge of the board.
      5. Land back on the board as it levels out.
